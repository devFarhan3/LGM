ABOUT LGM

Lotteries and Gaming Management is an application that seeks to bridge the gap between the Punters, Regulators and Licensed operators.

Our main focus is to educate Ugandans about the potential harmful effect of problem gambling and responsible gambling. LGM’s mission is to empower industry role-players to understand, identify and respond to incidents of problem gambling.

WHAT WE OFFER?

  • Access to help: Punters dealing with Problem Gambling will use the LGM App to access trained counselors, treatment programs and mentorship programs.

  • Latest news, webinars and blogs on the Gaming Industry locally and internationally.

  • Responsible gambling training manuals, self-exclusion
